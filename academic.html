Academics

We follow a holistic and child-friendly curriculum, focusing on foundational literacy, numeracy, life skills, and overall growth.

📘 Classes Offered

Nursery to Class 4, with age-appropriate teaching methods for every level.

🧠 Teaching Methodology

Play-based, activity-driven, and interactive learning environments for better understanding and retention.

📊 Assessment System

Regular evaluations through worksheets, oral interactions, and creative assignments. No harsh exams in early years.

🔬 Subjects Taught

English, Hindi, Math, EVS, Art & Craft, Music, Moral Science, GK, and basic Computer Skills.
