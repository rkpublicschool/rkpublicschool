Information for Parents

At R.K. Public School, we believe that parents are partners in their child's educational journey. Here's everything you need to know as a parent.

👪 Parent-Teacher Meetings

Regular PTMs are scheduled every term. Dates will be shared via circulars and SMS. Your involvement is key to your child’s success.

📚 Homework Guidelines

Our homework is age-appropriate and skill-based. Please support your child in completing it, but avoid doing it for them.

💬 Communication

For academic queries or concerns, parents may contact the class teacher via school diary or during PTMs. Urgent concerns can be addressed at the office.

🎒 Uniform & Attendance

Ensure your child wears proper uniform daily and maintains 90%+ attendance for eligibility in assessments and activities.
