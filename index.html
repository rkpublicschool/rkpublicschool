About Us

R.K. Public School, established in 2014, is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing the all-round development of students. With a strong focus on discipline, values, and academic excellence, we aim to build confident and responsible future citizens. Our qualified teachers, smart classrooms, and activity-based learning approach create an enriching environment for every child. Located in Asansol, West Bengal, R.K. Public School continues to grow as a trusted institution, shaping young minds for a brighter tomorrow.
