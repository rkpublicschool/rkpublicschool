Admissions

We welcome students from Nursery to Class 4. The admission process is transparent and merit-based.

📌 Admission Process:

  1. Visit the school campus or call us for inquiry.
  2. Fill out the admission form (available at school office or online).
  3. Submit required documents along with form.
  4. Attend a short interaction session with the child (Nursery/LKG/UKG) or entrance test (Class 1–4).
  5. Receive confirmation and pay the admission fee.

📝 Required Documents:

  • Birth Certificate
  • 2 Passport Size Photos
  • Previous Class Report Card (if applicable)
  • Address Proof

You can download the fee structure (PDF format).
